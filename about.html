Our Mission & History

With a rich history dating back to 1965, Cliff Valley School has established itself as a premier private school in Atlanta, GA. Our commitment to academic excellence, paired with our dedication to character development, has made us a beloved institution in the community.

Our teaching approach is centered around the idea that every child learns differently. Our expert faculty use a range of teaching methods to engage our students and bring learning to life. From hands-on activities to project-based learning, we strive to make every lesson an enjoyable and enriching experience.

At Cliff Valley School, we believe that a well-rounded education extends far beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular programs and character-building activities help our students develop essential life skills, such as teamwork, leadership, and empathy. From sports to music, art, and community service, we offer a wide range of activities that cater to every interest and talent.
