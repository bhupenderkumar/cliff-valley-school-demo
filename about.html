Our Mission & History

Established in 1965, Cliff Valley School has a rich history of providing a high-quality education to students in Atlanta. Our dedicated faculty and staff have a deep commitment to helping each child reach their full potential.

At Cliff Valley School, we believe in a balanced approach to education that combines academic rigor with opportunities for creativity, exploration, and socialization. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and promote deep understanding.

Our school community is dedicated to fostering a love of learning, a sense of responsibility, and a strong sense of character in each student. We offer a range of extracurricular activities and programs that promote teamwork, leadership, and social skills.
