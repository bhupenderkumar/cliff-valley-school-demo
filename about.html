Our Mission & History

Cliff Valley School has a rich history dating back to 1966, when it was founded by a group of local educators who shared a vision of providing a high-quality, private education to the children of Atlanta. Today, we continue to build on that legacy, offering a rigorous and well-rounded curriculum that prepares our students for success in high school and beyond.

At Cliff Valley, we believe that every child learns at their own pace and in their own way. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching approaches to engage and challenge our students, from hands-on projects and group activities to individualized instruction and online resources.

Beyond the classroom, Cliff Valley offers a wide range of extracurricular activities and programs that help our students develop important life skills like teamwork, leadership, and communication. From sports teams and clubs to community service projects and performances, there's something for every interest and talent.
