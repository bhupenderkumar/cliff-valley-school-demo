Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children who are not yet eligible for Kindergarten. We offer a play-based curriculum that focuses on social, emotional, and cognitive development, as well as introductory skills in reading, writing, and math.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-4 and is built around a balanced curriculum that includes reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students in grades 5-8 and offer a more challenging and specialized curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond.
